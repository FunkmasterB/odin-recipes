Bacon Leek Soup

Bacon potatoe leek soup is one of my absolute favorite soups. A hearty and filling meal thats sure to make the family happy

The creaminess and richness of the broth come together so well you'lll be reaching for bowl after bowl

  • 2 Tbs Unsalted butter
  • 2 Large Leeks
  • 1/2 Lbs Bacon
  • 4 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 Lbs Potatoes
  • 1/2 lb Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Thyme, Parsley and Salt to taste
  1. Dice potatoes into small cubes and add to pot with chicken broth and bring to boil, once boiling reduce to medium and simmer for 30 minutes or until potatoe crumbles.
  2. Cut leek and bacon into 1/4 In slices
  3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add bacon. stirring occainsally to avoid burning.
  4. When bacon is done cooking remove meat from pan and keep greace. Add the leek to the pan and fry the leek in bacon fat
  5. Once leek is fried, Add leek and bacon to potatoe pot, mix and addd the heavy cream.
  6. Slowly mix in the shredded cheese and cook for 10 more mintues, constantly sirring.
  7. Remove from heat and serve imeditaly
